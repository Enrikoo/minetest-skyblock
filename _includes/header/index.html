What is Skyblock?

Skyblock is a mod for Minetest that uses singlenode mapgen. This means the world is a complete void except for a starting island.

The player is given a list of quests to complete. When a quest is achieved the player will be given a reward. When all of the quests are achieved the player will move onto the next level.

The mod works in both single player as well as multi-player modes. In the multi-player mode each player is assigned to their own starting island.

Playing Instructions

Quest Block

Punch short left click to refresh your achievements.

Dig long left click to respawn at a new location with new items.

Open right click to view current achievements.

Death Hurts

If you die then you will have to restart the level and you will lose your items, however you will get to keep the structures you have created including chests.
